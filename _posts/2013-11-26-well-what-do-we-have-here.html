In the world of source-built, custom Android ROMs there is a specific milestone that all the developers aspire to, and those with ORD eagerly hunt for: the elusive "nightly" build. This elusive build is often rumored to be found in the hidden recesses of the Internet, but very often sightings are treated with heavy speculation and the occasional conspiracy theory. But not anymore!

After an announcement that we were stopping Android 4.3.x development and shifting our focus towards Android 4.4.x, and gearing up for nightlies, the excitement level and anticipation has been contagious. And now, thanks to the hard work put in by many of the Contributors, we can officially say that "YOU CAN HAZ NIGHTLIES!"

If you visit our Downloads page, you'll see a nice interface for navigating through our file structure. The devices currently receiving nightly status are:

  • find5 (Oppo Find 5)
  • flo (Nexus 7 Wi-Fi 2013)
  • grouper (Nexus 7 Wi-Fi 2012)
  • hammerhead (Nexus 5)
  • i777 (Samsung Galaxy SII AT&T)
  • i9100g (Samsung Galaxy SIIG)
  • i9300 (Samsung Galaxy SIII)
  • i9305 (Samsung Galaxy SIII LTE)
  • mako (Nexus 4)
  • n7000 (Samsung Galaxy Note)
  • n7100 (Samsung Galaxy Note 2)
  • t0lte (Samsung Galaxy Note 2 LTE)
  • t0lteatt (Samsung Galaxy Note 2 LTE AT&T)
  • t0ltetmo (Samsung Galaxy Note 2 LTE T-Mobile US)
  • tilapia (Nexus 7 3G 2012)
