Update: Gerrit has arisen once more from the ashes! We've also updated to Gerrit 2.9 during the time offline.

Unfortunately, due to our server hosts' datacenter not having suitable power redundancy in place we have had an outage on the Omni gerrit instance, which is taking a bit longer to recover from than expected.

We've got plenty of backups, and we're working to get it back up soon. Until then, here is a picture of a duckling, to entertain you while we hit the server with a spanner.
